Problem

Project Background

Strange as it was back in 2021, but the company did not have a mobile app for D2C shoppers. The Corona pandemic has led to E-commerce traffic explosion. Mobile traffic was growing especially fast, but the mobile website underperformed due to site speed issues and various UX problems, for example, missing personalization capabilities.

Goal

Conceptualize, design and launch a high-converting, scalable, mobile-first shopping app for the key global markets, including USA, Canada, United Kingdom, India & Japan.

Role

Connecting UX and Business

As Product UX Lead, I directed the design team from the app concept through its launch, shaping the app user experience, interface design and features.

Approach

Design Methods and Artifacts

  • Competitive analysis
  • User research: focus groups, user interviews
  • Information architecture, wireframes and user flows
  • Visual and UX design
  • Continuous iteration

Process and Team

Design and Development Workflow

A lightweight, agile process with short feedback loops was used to design, develop and continuously improve the app.

  1. Analysis and Discovery
  2. Design and Prototype
  3. Code
  4. Test and Improve
  5. Go Live and Improve Continuously
